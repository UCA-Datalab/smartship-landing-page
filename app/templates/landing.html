Maritime route optimization tool to define a safe route and reduce fuel consumption.

Features

In the world of maritime transport, there are major challenges to be solved in the coming years.

Environmental damage

Maritime traffic produces 2.5% of the planet's total CO2. The International Maritime Organization's goal is to reduce these emissions by 40% before 2030.

High cost

The price of fuel for a day's sailing is around $66000, so for long routes the price is excessive. For example, a route from Algeciras to Shanghai (36 days) costs $2.4M.

Crew safety

Throughout 2020, 195 cases of armed robbery by pirates were reported. This combined with possible weather problems, causes crews to be exposed to great dangers.
